Top albums 2017 #6

    published on December 10, 2017

    Strand of Oaks - Hard Love


    Hard Love is guilty of the crime of not beeing Heal, it’s predecessor. Few albums are Heal. Our best estimate suggests that only one album actually is Heal. Luckily greatness is not relative even though sometimes as of a trick of light it might seem to be. Give Hard Love a good ol’ listen and let yourself find out just how great an album it really is. Or just let us tell you how great, it’s the 6th best this year according to this list of ours. There. Settled.
