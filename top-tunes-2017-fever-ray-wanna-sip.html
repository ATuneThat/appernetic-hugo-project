Top tunes 2017 #7

    published on December 7, 2017

    Fever Ray - Wanna Sip


    Karin Dreijer hasn’t sounded this menacing in a while. Sure some of her lyrics are even more aggressive than this but the pure brutality of the soundscape she creates on Wanna Sip should make it impossible for the tune to be even remotely as catchy as it is. Wanna Sip demands to be heard in every possible way and we sure hope that people do listen. It’s self evident that this tune makes the top 10 where it holds down the 7th spot by the neck.
